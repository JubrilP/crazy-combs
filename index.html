Our story

A little house with a lot of heart

Crazy Combs has never felt like a "salon chain" experience — because it isn't one. We live in a sweet old house on Wake Forest Road, front porch and all, right where Historic Oakwood meets Mordecai. Neighbors stroll over for their appointments. First-timers get greeted by name — and usually by Orson, our salon dog, who takes his welcoming duties very seriously.

Inside, it's bright, a little bit floral, and always full of conversation. Our stylists are educators, color nerds, curl whisperers, and extension specialists who genuinely love what they do — and who'll remember what you talked about last visit. Whether you're in for a quick trim or the color change you've been nervous-excited about for months, you'll leave feeling like yourself, only shinier.

  • A true neighborhood salon — walkable from Oakwood and Mordecai, with free parking out front.
  • Educators on staff — our team includes Virtue Labs and Kevin Murphy educators who train other stylists.
  • Everyone's welcome — wheelchair accessible, walk-ins when we can, and Orson-approved.

Why we're a Virtue salon

This isn't a shelf we stock and forget. Traci is a Virtue Labs educator, we use it behind every chair, every day, and we love teaching clients how to keep their hair healthy long after they leave our porch.

  • Human keratin, nothing fake — Alpha Keratin 60ku® is keratin in its purest form. Your hair recognizes it and pulls it exactly where damage lives.
  • Independently tested — every formula vetted by third-party panels, stylists, and formulators before it earns a spot.
  • Clean formulas — free of parabens, sulfates, phthalates, and synthetic colors or dyes.
